What Standard brings to the table

PRICE STABILITY

Standard Protocol utilizes overcollaterization to achieve price stability of MTR, our stablecoin. This is unlike algorithmic stablecoins, wich just rely on its system for maintaining price stability without backed assets. This also enables interoperability of MTR with other cryptocurrencies.

DECENTRALIZED ORACLE SYSTEM

Standard protocol also brings an incentivized dedicated oracle system to the blockchain industry. Standard protocol's oracle system has its own consensus algorithm to reward oracle providers in a decentralized manner. Standard Protocol's oracle providers are elected by governance.

MARKET EFFICIENT LIQUIDATION

Standard Protocol makes liquidation available on Automated Market Makers on our dex involving MTR, our stablecoin. This is different from other collateralized stablecoins that use auctions. These auctions tend to be exclusive to the governance token holders and also require scripts to participate. This makes barrier to entry for normal users and leads to plutocracy in the ecosystem.

First Collateralized Rebasable Stablecoin

Standard Protocol is a Collateralized Rebasable Stablecoin(CRS) protocol for synthetic assets operating across the Polkadot ecosystem.

In addition to commonly used digital currency assets, Standard Protocol will also use oracles to bridge stocks, index funds, commodities and even legal currency funds from the traditional markets into the cryptocurrency markets.

Standard Protocol's vision is simple, to bridge the markets and offer seamless digital products that tap into both traditional markets as well as cryptocurrency markets.

MEET THE TOKENS

METER(MTR)

Meter(MTR) is the stablecoin which is generated posting a collateral. Holders can use MTR as a medium of exchange, to buy others assets, and to farm tokens within the Standard Protocol ecosystem by providing liquidity.

LITER(LTR)

Liter(LTR) is a liquidity provider token that represents a share of the AMM module. Similar to LP tokens in Uniswap, LTR can be burned in an AMM to receive deposited assets. LTR can also be used for STND yield farming.

STANDARD(STND)

Standard (STND) is the network and governance token for using Standard Protocol. Stability Fee generated form lending out MTR will be distributed among STND holder.
